Team Structure

The team is divided into two groups of subsystems, mechanical and electrical. Our 50 members may be working/living/contributing in any of these subteams.

  • group4 Executive Positions

    The electrical and software leads manage the electrical systems necessary for the car to function.

    The chief engineer takes care of the mechanical systems and manages topics ranging from aerodynamics to frame design and packaging.

    The captain monitors the progress of the enitre team. On the nontechnical side, the captain directs a business team and a publicity team.
