Team Structure

The team is divided into two groups of subsystems, mechanical and marketing. Our 35 members may be working/living/contributing in any of these subteams.

  • group2 Executive Positions
    • Executives
    • Team Leader

      Setting strategy and direction.Modeling and setting the team's culture, values, and behavior. Coordinating the different departments and ensuring constant communication within the team and with the university.

    • Chief Engineer

      Interface with leaders of other departments to reflect the input from these departments. Ensure appropriate checking & review of all documents/drawings. Work planning/forecast, progress monitoring and adherence to scheduled completion of engineering documents and goals. Coordinate the final assembly of the race-car.

DRT Info

Democritus Racing Team was founded in the winter of 2015 from the Production and Management Engineering department in the Democritus University of Thrace (Xanthe, Greece).The team consists of university undergraduate students full of passion and will, intend to represent the university in the international Formula SAE Series races.The team is housed in the PME building, where all the planning and organization happens.
