DRT Info

Democritus Racing Team was founded in the winter of 2015 from the Production and Management Engineering department in the Democritus University of Thrace (Xanthe, Greece).The team consists of university undergraduate students full of passion and will, intend to represent the university in the international Formula SAE Series races.The team is housed in the PME building, where all the planning and organization happens.
